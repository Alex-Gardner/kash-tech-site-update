Self-Service Reporting

Self-service Reporting (BI) empowers teams such as product developers, sales, finance, marketing, operations, and customers to answer data questions, with appropriate governance supported by IT and BI analysts. Self-service reporting provides autonomy in obtaining necessary information – it allows the user to build their own reports, rather than relying on those provided by a computer program, an I.T. department or a third party. And it is perhaps the single greatest tool utilized by any organization when it comes establishing a competitive advantage and growing your business. Self-service Reporting forms the foundation for real-time analysis and decision making, fostering a proactive attitude across your organization. This new attitude will increase the velocity of your business and generate greater employee satisfaction.
